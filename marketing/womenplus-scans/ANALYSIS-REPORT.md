- **FitLine PowerCocktail** (morning drink)

- **Optimalset = 170,30 € UVP/month** (with advisor discount available)

### USP 3: Triple Quality Certification
- **Kölner Liste®** — all 4 products independently tested for banned substances (required standard for professional athletes)
- **GMP (Good Manufacturing Practice)** — voluntary pharmaceutical-grade production standard
- **Made in Germany** — entire supply chain (research, development, production, quality control) in Speyer, Germany

### USP 4: 30-Day Full Money-Back Guarantee
> "Auch angefangene oder leere Verpackungen reichen aus. Keine Formulare. Keine Rechtfertigung. Kein Stress."
Even opened/empty packaging qualifies for a refund. No forms, no justification required.
